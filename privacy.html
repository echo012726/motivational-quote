Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Welcome to motivational-quote.org ("we," "us," or "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit our website, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. Please read it carefully.

Summary: We do not collect personal information directly. However, third-party services (including Google AdSense and Google Analytics) that operate on our site may collect data such as cookies and usage information in order to serve personalized advertisements and measure traffic. See the details below.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you choose to subscribe to our email newsletter, we collect your email address solely for the purpose of delivering motivational content to your inbox. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected by our third-party partners. This includes:

  • IP address (anonymized or truncated where required by law)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page
  • Referring URLs (how you arrived at our site)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • General geographic location (country/region level)

This information is collected through cookies and similar technologies managed by Google (see Section 3 below).

Locally Stored Data

Our site uses your browser's local storage to save your preferences (such as light/dark theme, favorite quotes, and daily streak count). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for basic site functionality such as remembering your preferences. They do not collect personally identifiable information.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site — which pages are most popular, how long people stay, and how they navigate. Google Analytics uses cookies to gather this data in an aggregated, anonymized form. You can learn more at policies.google.com/privacy.

Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We display advertisements through Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-6175161566333696). Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet. These cookies allow Google and its partners to:

  • Serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests
  • Limit the number of times you see the same ad
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Report aggregate statistics to advertisers

Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit: How Google uses data from sites that use its services.
